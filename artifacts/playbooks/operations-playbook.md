## Operations Playbook excerpt

**Triage** 

monitor key dashboards and p90 activation. If a location exceeds p90, open a ticket and follow the exceptions checklist.

**Cadence** 

Confirmation → 24h missing info prompt → 48h verification reminder → final notice at 7 days.

**Exceptions**

Manual update on owner reply; secure upload link for failed document uploads; pause changes for cohorts with high failure rates.

**Product launches** 

Route new menu or branded product launches to marketing with launch checklist and promotional plan.
