Operations Playbook excerpt
- Triage: monitor key dashboards and p90 activation. If a location exceeds p90, open a ticket and follow the exceptions checklist.
- Cadence: confirmation → 24h missing info prompt → 48h verification reminder → final notice at 7 days.
- Exceptions: manual update on owner reply; secure upload link for failed document uploads; pause changes for cohorts with high failure rates.
- Product launches: route new menu or branded product launches to marketing with launch checklist and promotional plan.
